Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for some tasty yum lasagna. It is made from a copied recipe from a random Website. I have not, nor will ever make this recipe. There are many steps to it and it seems like a lot of work. Odds are that I will just buy an already made lasagna from the freezer section of the grocery store.

This recipe might be as good as grandmas or taste like something you found in a dumpster. Good luck and may be the lasagna odds be ever in your favor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can of tomato paste
  • 12 lasagna noodle
  • many more ingredients than I am willing to list

Directions

  1. Cook the sausage and stuff together in a pan. Simmer over low heat for an hour or two or whatever, stirring occasionally.
  2. Boil noodles
  3. Slap it all together like an arts and crafts project.
  4. Top it with a boatload of cheese. Cheese hides all sins.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for an hour or so. Pull out before burnt.
