Pizza Party

Description

Time for everyone's favorite dinner game, Pizza Party! Here is a very basic recipe for making a pizza. Follow it, or don't. The choice is yours. You could always just call Domino's.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cardboard crust mix
  • 3 small tomatoes
  • 1 handful of forest fungus
  • 1 jar of pizza sauce
  • 1 bag of shredded cheese or two or three
  • whatever else you're hanker'in for on your za

Directions

  1. Make your crusty crust.
  2. Top with sauce.
  3. Top with other toppings.
  4. Bake it.
  5. Eat it and pretend you like it.
