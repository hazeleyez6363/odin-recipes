Spaghetti-O-My-Goodness

Description

Have you ever wanted to experiment with mixing gelatin and random things? Love the pairing of hotdogs and sketti? Like to make your guests say yuck? If you answered yes to any of the above, then this is the recipe for you!

Wow your friends with your cooking skills or lack there of with this real gem.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of wieners
  • 2 cans Spaghetti-O's
  • 2 packs gelatin
  • 1/4 cup Tomato Soup
  • 1/4 cup water

Directions

  1. Combined the water and the soup in a saucepan.
  2. Mix in the gelatin till it ain't lumpy no mo.
  3. Stir in the skettis
  4. Mash into a bundt pan and chuck 'er in the fridge overnight
  5. To serve, flip 'er over onto your fanciest serving dish and fill the middle of with chopped up wieners.
